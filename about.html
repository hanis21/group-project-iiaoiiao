ABOUT US


It is a Spanish frozen yogurt franchise brand founded in Denia in the province of Alicante, Spain.Hence, as of March 2016, llaollao is present in more than 180 establishments. Currently, llaollao is present in 25 countries on four continents including Europe and Latin America, Morocco, Australia, China, Russia, Cambodia, Malaysia, Singapore, Philippines, South Korea, Japan, United States and Brunei Darussalam.

In 16 countries around the world opened more than 150 shops.The first llaollao shop opened in 2009 and became a pioneer in selling frozen yogurt in Spain.The first store opened in the economic crisis. In June 2009 is still in the economic crisis, llaollao founder Pedro Espinosa left his job at British Petroleum in his 24 years old in Spain's Denia (Alicante) opened its first store, a originally designed to only 3 to 5 individuals composed of small family business tradition in the world of gastronomy. Denia's first store opened successfully for Espinosa, which is planning a commercial expansion to Madrid, Valencia and Spain.


By July 2015, llaollao had generated a total of 1,000 jobs during the five years that have passed since it launched its franchise model to the market. llaollao opened its biggest outlet in the world in August 2015 at Suntec City, Singapore. Beginning 2016, Llaollao will commence a 70 outlet expansion in Japan.


At the same time due to the inspiration of tourists, Espinosa also has the idea to open franchise and began to think about the basis of this model, he realized that the fastest speed must establish a good team to grasp the best time. In addition, llaollao set out the conditions of the franchise business is also very reasonable.


2010 to 2011 is the difficult period of llaollao, with the increase in the number of stores, for their investment to bring a heavy financial burden. But in the end and the franchisee's cooperation under the capital investment llaollao through the difficulties. Founder of the company attaches great importance to the brand image, its very healthy image is the key to the success of Llaollao, another key is the brand to provide the product healthy, natural and affordable. To the main color of green decoration shop successfully built its green image.


In any country, franchisees operate in exactly the same way as they are sold in Spain. They can be coordinated according to the characteristics and preferences of each country, llaollao always retain their original flavor. Currently, the company that incorporates healthy values (a medium-sized tub contains 46% of the Calcium Recommended Daily Allowance) and a fresh image, is a leader among its competitors. Singapore's Prime Minister, Lee Hsien Loong following his official visit to Madrid where he met Spain's King Felipe VI once referenced Llaollao and Zara as popular brands amongst around 200 Spanish companies present in Singapore.
